Past Workshops & Events

University Experience Critical Thinking Workshops

October 24 & 26, 2017

In late October, the Makerspace hosted two WKU University Experience classes for a hands-on critical thinking workshop. Students and staff made and debugged simple LED wearables.

Gatton Academy Student Visitation

September 6, 2017

In early September, the Makerspace demonstrated the X-Carve and 3D printers for around a dozen Gatton Academy students, some of whom became regular users of the space.

WKU Upward Bound Student Visitation

June 30, 2017

In late June, the Makerspace invited 29 students from the WKU Upward Bound program to tour the space and participate in an engaging morning of making. The staff assisted students with making marble fidget spinners and LED circuit bracelets.

2017 Bowling Green IdeaFestival

March 15, 2017

The WKU Makerspace staff joined representatives from LVL1 Hackerspace and Green River Region Educational Cooperative in creating an ad hoc makerspace at the 2017 Bowling Green IdeaFestival.
